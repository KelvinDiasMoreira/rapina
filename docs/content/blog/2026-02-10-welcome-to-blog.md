We're excited to launch the Rapina blog! This is where we'll share updates, insights, and knowledge with the community.

## What to Expect

Here's what you'll find on the Rapina blog:

### Release Notes
Stay up to date with the latest versions, features, and breaking changes. We'll provide detailed changelogs and migration guides to help you upgrade smoothly.

### Tutorials
In-depth guides and walkthroughs from both the core team and community contributors. Learn best practices, advanced patterns, and real-world solutions.

### RFCs & Design Decisions
Peek behind the curtain at our design process. We'll share Requests for Comments (RFCs) and discuss architectural decisions, giving you insight into why Rapina works the way it does.

### Tips & Patterns
Quick wins and practical patterns to make your development experience even better. From configuration tricks to performance optimizations, we'll share what we've learned.
